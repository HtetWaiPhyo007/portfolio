Historical Overview

Myanmar's history is one of resilience in the face of colonialism, internal conflict, and military rule. It has a rich cultural heritage, rooted in Buddhism and the legacy of its ancient kingdoms, but the country continues to struggle with political instability and ethnic divisions. The road to lasting peace and democracy remains challenging.
